About RAVINDRA.V.PAUSKAR

FOUNDER of EPICOPRODUCTIONS

RAVINDRA V PAUSKAR is originally from Dharwad and belongs to a middle class community. Father Mr. Vallabh P Pauskar, mother Mrs. Mangala Pauskar. RAVINDRA V PAUSKAR completed his childhood education at Pavan English medium school, Dharwad. Completed his PU education in Karnataka College Dharwad, He perused his degree in Electrical and Electronics at AGMR College, Varur. He completed his education life along with many of his achievements and projects.

In addition he has great knowledge of the diploma course A+, N+, CCNA, CCNP, MCITP, MCSA and many software programming languages along with this he guided many students through his classes conducted at EPICOP Computer Academy in sector of Srushti CADD Center. In 2015 he established his own company epicoproductions, its a ecommerce platform, he has successfully designed and developed numerous websites for social welfare, educational purpose, commercial sites. we appreciate his special capability to manage the characters of both roles as boss and employee i.e. he has worked as a employee in the following fields and technologies, he was first line manager and senior software testing engineer and worked as network engineer in Compucom as well as CEO of epicoproductions along with the employees under him. Although being an entrepreneur, he never ignored or compromised on his family business Ravindra Jewellaries located at sapthapur, Dharwad. He has achieved a certificate of diamond and gold jewellaries making course by Govt skill development scheme.
