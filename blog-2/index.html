Mr. Ravindra Vallabh Pauskar completed his education life along with many of his achievements and projects. He has conducted Robo-Mania event  during his college fest. It was special contribution for the college  from his side as it was the first time in entire college who has conducted such event, he made the participants to build their own robots and race them with the opponents robot, it gave others inspiration to innovate  and execute their ideas. He gave complete concentration and dedication for his final year project i.e. “Vertical Axis Buoyant Air Turbine” which was innovation for electric power generation. He never stopped his innovations along with hardware experiments he began to build his own software applications .
